Privacy Policy App: Household Budget Planner Developer: TS Essentials Contact: tsessentials2020@gmail.com Effective Date: April 18, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how TS Essentials ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Household Budget Planner Android application ("the App"). 1. Information We Collect We collect the following data when you use the App: Email address — collected when you sign in with Google, used to identify your account and sync your data across devices. User ID — a unique identifier assigned by Firebase when you sign in, used to associate your data with your account. Purchase history — subscription and purchase information processed by RevenueCat to validate your subscription tier and restore purchases. App interactions — basic usage data collected by Firebase to help us understand how the App is used and improve performance. Device identifiers — used by RevenueCat to manage subscription state across devices. Crash logs — collected by Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix bugs. 2. How We Use Your Information To authenticate your account via Google Sign-In To sync your budget data across devices (Standard and Pro tiers) To validate and restore your subscription entitlements To diagnose crashes and improve app stability 3. Third-Party Services The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies: Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy RevenueCat (Subscription management) — revenuecat.com/privacy Google Play Billing — Google Payments Privacy Notice 4. Data Storage and Security Your data is stored securely in Google Firestore and transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties beyond the services listed above. 5. Data Retention and Deletion Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by submitting a request here: Submit an Account Deletion Request Upon receiving your request, we will delete your Firebase account and associated Firestore data within 30 days. 6. Children's Privacy The App is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. 7.
